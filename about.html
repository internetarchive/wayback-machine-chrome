The Official Wayback Machine Extension

In cooperation with Google Summer of Code, The Internet Archive presents The Official Wayback Machine Extension. With the power of the Wayback Machine, we let you go back in time to see how a URL has changed and evolved through the history of the Web!

Features

  • 404 Not Found?

    Website no longer available? Not a problem! This will popup a link to the archived website, automatically.

  • Save Page Now

    Instantly save the page you are currently viewing in The Wayback Machine. Outlinks saves even more pages and may take longer. Check Email Outlinks in Settings to receive a list of links. Check Auto Save Page to save pages that have yet to be archived, automatically!

  • Oldest, Overview, & Newest

    View the first version of a page or the most recently saved in the Wayback Machine. Or view a calendar overview of all archived pages.

  • Site Map

    Draws a sunburst diagram or radial tree structure for every year that the website has been archived.

  • Related Tweets & Share Links

    Searches Twitter for information regarding your current page. Share Wayback links on social media.

  • Show Contexts

    Provides a page of a variety of information regarding the page you are viewing, including analytics from Alexa, capture summary from the Wayback Machine, annotations from Hypothes.is, and a word tag cloud.

  • Relevant Archived Resources

    Display archived resources on relevant URLs, including from Amazon books, Wikipedia, and various news sources. This feature will let you view digitized books and papers cited on the website if the Internet Archive has a copy!

  • Wayback Count

    Display a count of pages that have been archived each time you visit a website.
